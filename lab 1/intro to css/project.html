Travel to cheesecake

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest, layer consists of a mixture of a soft, fresh cheese typically

  • cottage cheese
  • cream cheese
  • ricotta
  • eggs
  • sugar

About cheesecake

If there is a bottom layer, it most often consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies (or digestive biscuits), graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake. Cheesecake may be baked or unbaked (and is usually refrigerated).


Cheesecake is usually sweetened with sugar and may be flavored in different ways.
  1. Vanilla
  2. spices
  3. lemon
  4. chocolate
  5. pumpkinor
Additional flavors and visual appeal may be added by topping the finished dessert with fruit, whipped cream, nuts, cookies, fruit sauce, chocolate syrup or other ingredients.
